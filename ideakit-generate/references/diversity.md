# Structural diversity diagnostic

Use this only when concepts converge. Diversity is evidence that the search space was explored, not a
portfolio quota. Never retain a weak concept to represent a cell.

## Diagnose sameness

Two concepts are effectively duplicates when they share most of:

- actor and triggering situation;
- job/desire and promised progress;
- value mechanism;
- payer and business architecture;
- acquisition wedge;
- source thesis and causal chain.

Renaming a feature, changing the industry noun, or swapping “app” for “platform” does not create a new
venture.

## Axes that can open the space

Select 2–4 axes relevant to the brief:

- actor: user, buyer, beneficiary, gatekeeper;
- value: pain, obligation, identity, ritual, status, belonging, access;
- mechanism: automation, coordination, verification, financing, education, creation, discovery;
- architecture: product, service-first, infrastructure, network, marketplace, media/community,
  standard/data asset;
- payer: user, employer, institution, sponsor, transaction participant, public funder;
- wedge: manual concierge, community, integration, report, tool, service, local density;
- time: works now, depends on a near-future capability, becomes valuable after a second-order shift;
- force effect: direct response, changed behavior, rearranged structure, new bottleneck;
- who is displaced: incumbent vendor, internal labor, agency, spreadsheet/chat workaround, nobody
  (non-consumption).

Map current concepts quickly. Regenerate only where the map reveals an important unexplored thesis or
mechanism. Do not force consumer/B2B, pain/desire, or ring quotas when evidence and founder fit point
elsewhere. If the user asked for a broad search, explain any deliberate concentration.

## Quality over coverage

For each proposed addition ask:

1. Would it survive without the diversity exercise?
2. Does it have its own Observed → Inferred → Bet chain?
3. Does it change the venture mechanism or merely the packaging?
4. Is it more informative than deepening an existing thesis?

If not, leave the cell empty.
