# Clinic scheduling concierge — validation brief

Observed: two clinic managers showed their existing night-shift handoff logs ([source](https://example.com/evidence)).
Inferred: the handoff, not message volume alone, creates missed bookings. Bet: a manager will delegate
one shift if escalation remains under clinic control.

Recommendation: reframe toward a managed night shift rather than software-only automation.
Evidence level: E1 Behavior — observed workaround, not willingness to pay. Next costly signal: one
manager shares a live shift and signs a paid-pilot condition.

Wedge: one clinic, one channel, one night shift. Pilot flow includes manual escalation. Kill criteria:
park if no clinic shares live data or if escalation consumes more labor than the fee supports.
