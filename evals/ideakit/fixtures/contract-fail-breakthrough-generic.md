## Business productivity hub

**Observed:** A placeholder business performs repetitive work. [Synthetic source](https://example.com/productivity)

**Inferred:** Businesses want easier workflows.

**Bet:** Small businesses will pay for convenience. Wrong if free tools are sufficient.

Revelation: An AI-powered platform can put every business workflow in one place.

Why others miss it: The market is fragmented and nobody has combined all the features yet.

Solo entry: Start with a paid pilot, one manual onboarding flow, and an affordable-loss cap.

Value capture: Small businesses pay a subscription for convenient access to the platform.

Paid commitment: One small business prepays a monthly subscription before onboarding.

Delivered value: The subscriber opens the all-in-one dashboard after onboarding.

Killer risk: Adoption may be slow; disconfirm the idea if the pilot does not convert.

The wedge is one workflow. First-10 distribution uses a named local business association. The counter-case is incumbent bundling. The cheapest test is a clickable prototype, and usage data may compound into power.
