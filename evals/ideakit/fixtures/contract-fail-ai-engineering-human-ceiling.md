# AI-powered task manager

Observed: Remote teams use task software.

Inferred: They may want summaries.

Bet: AI can summarize tasks.

Because you are solo, limit the product to one feature that you can code after work. It must start as
a manual service and should never attempt the larger workflow system a conventional team would build.

Revelation: An AI-powered platform summarizes tasks.

Why others miss it: AI is new.

Solo entry: Build the feature on a weekend.

Value capture: Charge a subscription.

Paid commitment: Ask someone to pay.

Delivered value: Show a summary.

Killer risk: People may not want it.

Wedge: A summary button.

First-10 distribution: Do marketing.

Affordable-loss test: Spend one weekend building it.
